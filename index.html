ABOUT ME

I am currently working on a project entitled "Global Lagomorph Macroecology: interspecific interactions and the impact of climate change". I have explored a variety of ecological questions relating to the global distribution of lagomorphs, a widespread mammalian group comprising rabbits, hares and pikas. I have used many techniques, including species distribution modelling, literature reviews, phylogenetic analyses and ecological network theory, to advance knowledge on this group of species.

I also help run the Queen's University Belfast School of Biological Sciences Blog: QUBio. You can access the blog here or by clicking the tab above.

Research interests

  • macroecology
  • species distribution modelling
  • climate change
  • biotic interactions
  • network analysis
  • biodiversity conservation
